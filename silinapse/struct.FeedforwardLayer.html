A feedforward layer

Such layer is composed of a set of output neurons, and have all its inputs connected to all its outputs.

The effective computation is thus, if X is the vector of inputs, Y the vector of outputs, W the internal weigths matrix, B the vector of biases and f() the activation function (applied on all components of the vector in parallel):

Y = f( W*X + B )

The training of this layer consists on fitting the values of W and B.
